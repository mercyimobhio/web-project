About Mercy Imobhio

I am a highly skilled and detail-oriented Orthopedic Technician with 2 years of experience in clinical settings. My expertise includes applying and removing various casts, fitting braces, assisting orthopedic surgeons during procedures, and educating patients on post-operative care.

I am committed to maintaining sterile environments and ensuring patient comfort and safety
